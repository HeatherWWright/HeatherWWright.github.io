About Me

My name is Heather Wright and I am a 5th grade teacher at Independence Elementary. I graduated with my Bachelors of Science in Elementary Education with a minor in Literacy. I am certified to teach elementary grades 1-6 in Missouri state. I am a candidate for the Learning Technologies and Design masters program through the University of Missouri Online with an anticipated graduation date in the summer of 2025.
I am happily married to my husband David. We have 3 beautiful children, a dog named Cosmo, and 3 tortoises; Baby, Ted The Hero, and Das Machine. I love reading, traveling, and playing video games.
