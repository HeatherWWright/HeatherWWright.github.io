Welcome!

Welcome 5th grade mathematicians and families! This website will serve as a resource for you to see what tools and strategies we are learning in the classroom. It will also provide extra practice and guidance over our math topics.

Course Goals

Students will use the 9 mathematical practices in order to learn the order of operations and apply them to real world situations. We will discuss why order matters and how to apply them to solve problems.

Course Objectives

  • Write, evaluate, and interpret numerical expressions using the order of operations.
  • Solve and justify multi step problems involving variables and whole numbers.
  • Explain our thinking using examples
